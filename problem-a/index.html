Frodo Baggins

Frodo Baggins is a young Hobbit from the Shire who enjoys eating and smoking pipeweed and spending time with his friends Samwise, Merry, and Pippin. He is Bilbo Baggins cousin and adopted son. At the behest of the wizard Gandalf, Frodo eventually embarks on a long and grand journey to destroy the One Ring along with those chosen to be in the fellowship of the ring. He is the carrier of the ring as he is deemed the one who can manage to hold the ring without being fully corrupted. In his journey he overcomes otherworldly evils and strange forces that try to stop him at every chance. His travels are documented in the Lord of The Rings trilogy of books written by J.R.R Tolkien. More information can be found in the Lord of the Rings wikipedia page . Some reasons I admire Frodo are listed below.

  • He makes excellent food and appreciates the importance of breakfast
