Frodo Baggins

Frodo is a Hobbit from the Shire. He enjoys eating and smoking pipeweed and spending time with his friends. He is Bilbo Baggins cousin and adopted son. He has a best friend named Samwise Gamgee and is also friends with the wizard Gandalf. He eventually embarks on a long and grand journey to destroy the One Ring along with the fellowship of the ring. He is the carrier of the ring as he is deemed the one who can carry the ring without being corrupted. His journeys are documented in the Lord of The Rings trilogy written by J.R.R Tolkien. More information can be found in the Lord of the Rings wikipedia page . Some reasons I admire Frodo are listed below.

  • He has really impressive long distance hiking skills
  • He is a Great cook
  • He is a really nice guy
  • He helps throw really fun parties with great magic tricks
