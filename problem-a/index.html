Frodo Baggins

Frodo is a Hobbit from the Shire. He enjoys eating and smoking pipeweed and spending time with his friends. He is Bilbo Baggins cousin and adopted son. He has a best friend named Samwise Gamgee and is also friends with the wizard Gandalf. He eventually embarks on a long and grand journey to destroy the One Ring along with the fellowship of the ring. He is the carrier of the ring as he is deemed the one who can carry the ring without being corrupted. His journeys are documented in the Lord of The Rings Book Series and more information can be found at the Lord of the Rings wikipedia page
